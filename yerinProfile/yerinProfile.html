Yerin Beak

R&B

Introduction

Yerin Beak is a South Korean singer and songwriter, born on March 26, 1997. She was also a member of the music duo 15&, and was one of them. Baek is known for her musical talent, and her music style encompasses many genres, including R&B, soul, and pop. She has gained popularity with albums such as "What allowed me to love and hate" and "Square (2017)" and has won several music awards. Baek also supports independent musicians by establishing her own music label, Blue Vinyl. Many people love her music, and her activities continue to receive attention.
